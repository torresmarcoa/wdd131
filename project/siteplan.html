Site name: CompuEssentials

I chose the name CompuEssentials because this website will talk about the brief history of computers and essential tips and components

Site Purpose:

This site provides information and a brief history of computers and their components. It offers essential tips, practical guides for users of all levels to help them understand and make informed decisions about technology.

Scenarios:

What are the basic components of a computer, and what do they do?"

What components should I focus on upgrading, and how do I choose the right ones?
