About Me

Hello! My name is Marco Antonio Torres Aceves. I was born in Texcoco, Mexico, and currently reside in Tuxtla Gutierrez, Chiapas, Mexico. I am 25 years old and have been a lifelong member of the Church. I had the privilege of serving a mission in the Mission Mexico Xalapa.

I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in software development, a field that truly excites me. My passion for technology encompasses all things related to computers, from providing technical support to programming. In my free time, I enjoy playing video games, reading books, writing, and staying updated on the latest technological advancements.

One of the aspects I deeply appreciate about studying at BYU-Idaho is the integration of spiritual and secular education. Here, I have learned to see them not as separate entities but as complementary areas that enrich my life. I have come to understand that I can rely on the Lord's guidance and support in all aspects of my life, whether they are spiritual, academic, or personal.

Looking ahead, I aspire to leverage my skills in software development to contribute to innovative projects that can make a positive impact on the world. I am excited about the opportunities ahead and am grateful for the strong foundation my education and faith provide.
